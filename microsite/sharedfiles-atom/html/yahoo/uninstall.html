Uninstall

Follow the instructions below to uninstall the extension from your Chrome™ browser

  1. Open your Chrome™ browser
  2. To access the Settings menu, click the three horizontal lines or three dots in the top right corner of the browser
  3. From the drop-down menu navigate to Extensions> Manage Extensions
  4. Locate the extension you want to remove and click ‘Remove’. To hide the extension without removing it, unselect the toggle button
  5. Restart your Chrome™ browser
